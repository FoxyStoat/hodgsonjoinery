Welcome to Hodgson Joinery

With over 30 years of experience in the carpentry trade, Hodgson Joinery is based in and around the Whitby area and provides craftmanship for the domestic and commercial markets. In need of a beautiful bespoke staircase, kitchen installation, plastering, an attic renovation or any other skilled woodwork and or a locksmith? Look no further! No job too big or small.
